'Stay Safe Online' Checklist



1\. Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.

2\. Do not click on unknown or suspicious links.

3\. Use strong passwords and enable two factor authentication.

4\. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions.

5\. Always remember that entering a UPI PIN means you are sending money, not receiving it.

6\. Install apps only from trusted sources like the Google Play Store.

7\. Regularly check your bank account for any unusual transactions.

8\. Set transaction limits on your UPI apps to reduce risk.

9\. Be careful while scanning QR codes from unknown sources.

10\. Do not forward suspicious messages to others.
