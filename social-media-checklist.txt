 Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

 Do's:
1. Think before posting anything online.
2. Be respectful and polite in interactions.
3. Share accurate and verified information.
4. Use social media for learning.
5. Use strong passwords to keep your account safe.
6. Keep your profile information updated.

Don'ts:
1. Do not spread fake news.
2. Do not share personal details.
3. Avoid posting offensive or harmful content.
4. Don't trust everything you see online.
5. Don't click on unknown links or messages.
6. Avoid using abusive language online.
